Frequently asked questions

What happens if I don't check in?
+
Your Dead Man's Switch will trigger after your set inactivity period (30-365 days), notifying your executors. They can then access your vault according to your pre-set instructions.
How secure is my data?
+
We use zero-knowledge encryption, meaning we literally cannot access your data. Your vault is encrypted with AES-256, the same standard used by governments and banks worldwide.
Can executors change my instructions?
+
No. Executors have read-only access to your vault and can only see what you've explicitly shared with them. All access is logged and auditable.
What if I want to update my will?
+
You can update your will, assets, and executor access anytime. Changes take effect immediately, and your executors are notified of any important updates.
Is there a free trial?
+
Yes! Start with our free Starter plan that includes 1 will, 5 assets, and 2 executors. Upgrade to Premium anytime for unlimited access.
